The Apache Flink community is excited to announce the release of Flink Kubernetes Operator 1.15.0! This release brings Kubernetes-native Conditions to FlinkDeployment, Logback logging support, bundled metric reporters, Flink 2.2 compatibility, and a number of important reliability fixes across session jobs, savepoints, and the mutating webhook. We encourage you to download the release and share your experience with the community through the Flink mailing lists or JIRA!
